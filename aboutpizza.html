Our Pizza Types

Meat Lover Pizza

It is one of our most popular pizza types according to our research by customers order. This pizza type includes a variety of meats, such as tender beef, sausage, chicken, pepperoni, and ham. Meat pizza is favoured by majority of Ghanaians.

BBQ Pizza

BBQ pizza is the finest pizza for people who can handle hot food! Seasoned pork, some vegetables, and barbecue sauce are all included in the Marie's BBQ pizza. This BBQ Pizza is delicious and spicy with lots of toppings. It is confirmed that the majority of consumers place orders for these.

All-Seasons Pizza

This pizza has all the toppings our pizza is ready to offer for the finest experience. All-seasons pizza is a combination of meat and vegetables. Additionally, they have ten (10) different toppings, which includes a variety of meat, vegetables, and even fruits.These pizzas are widely regarded as the best pizza, which is not surprising why people love this.

Our Special Pizza
